The Signal

What if passion isn't something you find?

Passion is not emotion. Not motivation. Not a personality trait you either have or don't.

Within The Passion Company, passion is approached as something more fundamental — the animating movement of human aliveness itself. The energy through which potential becomes lived experience.

You don't need to generate it. It's already present. The question isn't "How do I become more passionate?"  but rather:  "What conditions influence how clearly aliveness moves through experience?"

TPC does not engineer people.

TPC builds environments in which individuals may engineer their own relationship to experience.

The Investigators

Built by investigators, not instructors

The Passion Company was co-founded by Jeremy Lasman and Amber Sheets — not as experts delivering solutions, but as ongoing investigators participating alongside everyone who engages with the work.

Jeremy brings a background in technology and systems thinking, including work at SpaceX, where precision engineering meets the frontier of human ambition. Amber contributes deep experience in experiential exploration and the investigation of awareness within human systems.

Together, they approach this work as a shared laboratory — developing frameworks through lived experimentation rather than prescribing answers from authority. Knowledge within TPC develops through participation, not hierarchy.
