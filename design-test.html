11 / Accessibility

WCAG AA Contrast Verification

Every text-on-surface combination tested against WCAG AA requirements. Body text requires 4.5:1. Large text (18px+/14px bold) requires 3:1. Ratios are computed live and update when toggling between light and dark mode.
